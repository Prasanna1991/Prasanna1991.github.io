About

I am a second year PhD student in B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am working as a research assistant at Computational Biomedicine Lab (CBL) which is directed by my advisor, Dr. Linwei Wang. My research is devoted in disentangling factors of variations in the data to improve predictability. I am interested in applying deep learning techniques to biomedical data sets like Electrocardiogram (ECG) .

In November 2014, I graduated as Electronics and Communication Engineer from Institute of Engineering, Tribhuwan University, Nepal.

Research Interests: Factor disentanglement, Representation Learning, Deep Learning, HealthAI.

Timeline events:

  • May 2016: Our new paper "Disentangling Inter-Subject Variations: Automatic Localization of Ventricular Tachycardia Origin from 12-Lead Electrocardiograms" was accepted to ISBI 2017.

  • August 2016: Joined Rochester Institute of Technology for PhD in Computing and Information Sciences
