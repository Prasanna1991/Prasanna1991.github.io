I am a second PhD student in B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am working as a research assistant at Computational Biomedicine Lab (CBL) which is directed by my advisor, Dr. Linwei Wang. My research is focused in disentangling factors of variations in the data to improve predictability. I am interested in applying deep generative models to biomedical data sets like Electrocardiogram (ECG) . In November 2014, I graduated as Electronics and Communication Engineer from Institute of Engineering, Tribhuwan University, Nepal.
